Editor's Choice

Miso-Glazed Salmon with Wilted Greens

The Elements
  • Fresh Salmon Fillet
  • Baby Spinach (2 cups)
  • Lemon Zest & Juice
  • White Miso Paste
Culinary Protocol
01

Whisk miso, lemon juice, and a touch of honey. Pat the salmon dry and sear skin-side down for 4 minutes until crisp.

02

Flip and glaze generously. Cook for 2 more minutes. Toss the baby spinach in the same pan for 30 seconds until just wilted.
