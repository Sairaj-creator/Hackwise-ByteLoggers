Miso-Glazed Atlantic Salmon Bowl

Chef's Selection • 25 Mins

The Dish Identity

"A sophisticated balance of umami-rich miso and fresh, vibrant greens. This recipe utilizes high-tech flavor layering techniques to maximize nutrient absorption while maintaining a restaurant-quality aesthetic."

540 Calories
42g Protein
12g Fiber
18g Net Carbs

The Elements

2 SERVINGS
Fresh Atlantic Salmon 2 Fillets
White Miso Paste 2 tbsp
Organic Quinoa 1 cup
Ripe Avocado 1 unit
Baby Spinach 2 cups
Honey / Maple Syrup 1 tsp

Culinary Protocol

1

Glaze Synthesis

Whisk miso paste, honey, and a splash of warm water until a smooth emulsion is achieved. Coat salmon thoroughly.

2

Precision Sear

Preheat skillet to medium-high. Sear salmon skin-side down for 4 minutes until golden. Flip and glaze.

3

Foundation Assembly

Divide cooked quinoa into chilled ceramic bowls. Layer with spinach and sliced avocado.

4

Final Integration

Place salmon atop the base. Drizzle remaining glaze and top with sesame seeds.
